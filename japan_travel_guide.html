Map and Itinerary

Attractions

  • Akihabara Anime Pilgrimage: Visit Akihabara Radio Kaikan. Recommended visiting on weekdays. For more details, visit here.
  • Kendo Experience in Tokyo: Located at Tokyo Kendo Center. Sessions are from 7 PM to 9 PM on Tuesdays and Thursdays. Book your place here.
  • Tea Ceremony in Kyoto: At Kimono Tea Ceremony Kyoto MAIKOYA. Check the schedule and book here.
  • Zen Meditation in Kyoto: Shunkoin Temple within Myoshin-ji temple complex. Sessions are from 10:00 AM to 11:30 AM daily. Book through their official website.
  • Todaiji Temple and Deer Park in Nara: Open from 7:30 to 17:30 (or 18:00 in summer). Admission fee is 600 yen. To get there, take a bus from Kintetsu Nara Station or JR Nara Station to 'Daibutsuden Kasuga Taisha-mae' bus stop. More info here.

Basic Japanese Phrases

  • Hello: こんにちは (Konnichiwa)
  • Thank you: ありがとう (Arigatou)
  • Excuse me: すみません (Sumimasen)
  • Goodbye: さようなら (Sayounara)

Travel Tips

  • Always carry cash as not all places accept credit cards.
  • Be respectful of local customs and traditions, especially when visiting temples and shrines.
  • Try to learn some basic Japanese phrases; it can greatly enhance your experience.
  • Use public transportation for efficient and affordable travel between cities and attractions.
